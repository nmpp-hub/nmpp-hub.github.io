In this work, the stability of ion Bernstein waves (IBWs) is extensively studied in various conditions. Analytical investigations of the dispersion relation are combined with numerical simulations with a novel 6D kinetic Vlasov code. In addition, the energy fluxes induced by such waves have been investigated. The analysis shows the relevance of high-frequency waves in regimes of steep gradients, commonly found in the plasma edge of fusion devices.
