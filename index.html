Working groups

The division comprises six working groups:

  • Advanced Computing Hub (ACH)
  • Finite Element Methods (FEM)
  • Geometric Numerical Integration and Reduced Complexity Modelling (GNI)
  • Kinetic and Gyrokinetic Models (KGKM)
  • Magnetohydrodynamics (MHD)
  • Probabilistic Data Analysis and Active Learning (DAAL)
  • Zonal Flows in Turbulent Plasmas (ZFSFTP)
